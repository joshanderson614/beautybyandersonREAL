Our Story

Beauty by Anderson was established in 1995 in Westwood, Los Angeles. For over 25 years, we have been providing premium hair styling, cuts, and treatments to our loyal clients. What started as a small neighborhood salon has grown into a destination for clients who value quality, consistency, and a relaxing atmosphere.

Our Philosophy

We believe in combining artistry with care. Every client receives personalized attention and premium, non-toxic products. We are committed to making you look and feel your best—without compromise on quality or your hair's health.

Our Expertise

We specialize in precision cuts, signature blow-dries, and restorative treatments like our Milbon deep conditioning. Our stylists stay current with techniques and trends while honoring classic, timeless results. We use only professional-grade, non-toxic formulas.
